Our Story

Flour, time,
and tradition.

La Mie Dorée was born from a single obsession: the perfect loaf. We use stone-milled heritage grains, long cold ferments, and wood-fired ovens to create bread the way it was meant to be made — slowly, with care.

Every morning before dawn, our bakers shape the doughs that have been resting overnight. By the time the city wakes, the aroma of fresh bread already fills the street.
